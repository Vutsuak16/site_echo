FAQ

Q

What is Echo?

Echo is a mobile app built with the aim of enabling its users to make new connections, and leverage their existing social circles to achieve something substantial. Through Echo you can discover people around you based on the connections you share and professional/personal interests

Q

Can anyone track my location using Echo?

No. No one can track your location using Echo. Echo just uses a person’s real time location and shows you who is nearby.

Q

What privacy settings does Echo provide?

Echo ensures that a user has the final say in choosing against issues concerning their privacy. It offers its users the choice to stay hidden on the app. If you don’t want anyone to find you on the app, you just have to disable the feature ‘Let others discover me’ in the Settings. Even the chat in Echo runs on a request mechanism.

Q

Will Echo drain away my battery?

Echo is built in a manner to minimize any kind of battery drain. Our app is optimized to show its users the real time data even when the locations services are running on’batterty saving mode. Echo only accesses Wi-Fi networks or the nearest mobile tower data to narrow down the location of the users. With this feature in place, the impact on your phone’s battery will hardly be noticeable..
